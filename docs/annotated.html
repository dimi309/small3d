 CBoundingBoxSetSet of bounding boxes for a SceneObject, normally loaded from a Wavefront file, or calculated based on the vertices of a Model. In order to create a BoundinBoxSet in Blender for example (see blender.org), just place the boxes in the preferred position over a model.
